Tree in the Middle of the Road

Iowa is also home to the famous “Tree in the Middle of the Road.” Rivaling the likes of the Eiffel Tower, Taj Mahal, and Statue of Liberty, this tree that is in the middle of the road will have you saying, “Wow, that’s a pretty big tree in the middle of the road.” In fact, this may be the biggest tree in the middle of the road that you’ve ever seen. The tree, located in Brayton, is over 170 years old and 100 feet tall, and is in fact growing in the middle of a dirt road. A top attraction in the state, you will be in shock over how big the tree in the middle of the road is. The tree in the middle of the road is an amazing sight, and something that can only be found in the great state of Iowa.
