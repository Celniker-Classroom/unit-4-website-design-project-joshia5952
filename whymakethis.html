Why make this?

One may be thinking, who in their right mind would decide to make this a website? Who would be insane enough to dedicate their limited time to study the pastimes of the Iowan people despite seemingly having no affiliation with them at all? Well, I have an answer to that question. Previously, I lived in the Midwest, Indiana, to be exact, where my family was driving through the state of Iowa, where we stumbled upon an interesting landmark on the side of the road similar to those shown on this webpage. Remembering that, I had the idea of making this webpage in order to showcase some silly landmarks I found on the internet. Everything on this project is intended as satire, and all content should be taken with a truckload of salt, as I hardly recall this website having any worthwhile information. Thanks for reading!
