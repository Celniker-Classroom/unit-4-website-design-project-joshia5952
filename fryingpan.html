Visit Iowa

Iowa is home to one of the world’s largest frying pans, measuring roughly 14 feet. Located in Brandon, this frying pan will have you in awe over its frying capabilities. With this much fryerpower, you can fry corn to your heart’s content. The creativity of the Iowan people is unmatched, and this landmark is proof of that. The 1500 pound frying pan was built in 2004 to promote Brandon’s annual “Cowboy Breakfast” fundraiser. Since then, the frying pan has become the pride and joy of all 300 people living in Brandon, and showcases the grit and determination of the Iowan people.
